Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about solar energy and our services

The amount you save depends on several factors including your current electricity usage, the size of your solar system, your roof's sun exposure, and local energy rates. On average, our customers save between $15,000 to $35,000 over the 25-year lifespan of their solar system. Many homeowners see monthly savings of $100 to $300 or more. In Las Vegas, with our abundant sunshine and high electricity rates, the savings potential is exceptional. During your free energy audit, our experts will provide a detailed analysis showing your specific savings projections based on your home's unique characteristics.

Solar panels still generate electricity on cloudy days, though at reduced efficiency—typically 25-50% of their sunny day output. Thanks to Las Vegas's 300+ days of sunshine annually, cloudy days are relatively rare. Additionally, most homes remain connected to the grid, so you automatically draw power from the utility company when your panels aren't producing enough. Modern net metering programs allow excess energy you generate to be credited back to your account, and you can draw from those credits when needed. This ensures you always have reliable power regardless of weather conditions. Many customers also choose to add battery storage systems for additional energy independence and backup power during outages.

Solar panels require minimal maintenance, which is one of their biggest advantages. Dust and debris can occasionally accumulate, reducing efficiency by 5-10%, but rain naturally cleans the panels in most cases. We recommend a professional cleaning 1-2 times per year for optimal performance. We offer maintenance packages that include regular inspections, cleaning, and monitoring to ensure your system operates at peak efficiency. Our team checks electrical connections, inverter performance, and overall system health. Most customers find that the minimal maintenance costs are far outweighed by the energy savings. Additionally, all components come with comprehensive warranties covering defects and performance issues for 25 years.

We offer multiple financing options to fit every budget and situation. You can purchase your system outright and take advantage of the federal Investment Tax Credit (currently 30%) plus Nevada state incentives. Solar loans allow you to finance your system while still receiving tax credits and rebates. Solar leases require no upfront costs, though you receive lower savings since the leasing company owns the system. Power Purchase Agreements (PPAs) let you pay only for the electricity your system generates. Many customers also qualify for low-interest financing through our partner lenders. Our financial advisors will help you compare options and find the best solution for your circumstances. Most customers see positive cash flow from day one with their chosen financing method.

The entire process from initial consultation to system activation typically takes 4-8 weeks. The free energy audit and proposal usually happen within 3-5 days. Once you approve the proposal, we handle all paperwork, permits, and inspections with local authorities. The actual installation typically takes 1-3 days depending on system size and roof complexity. After installation, our team performs comprehensive testing and connections to the grid. You'll receive training on system monitoring and maintenance. In Nevada, we have established relationships with permitting authorities, which helps expedite the process. We keep you informed at every stage and work efficiently to minimize disruption to your home and daily life.
